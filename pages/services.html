Our Services

Clear, specialized care delivered with modern facilities and trusted doctors.

General Surgery

Our General Surgery department provides comprehensive surgical care using both traditional and minimally invasive techniques. Procedures are performed with a focus on safety, faster recovery, and optimal outcomes.

  • Appendectomy: Surgical removal of the appendix, commonly performed in cases of acute appendicitis.
  • Cholecystectomy: Removal of the gallbladder to treat gallstones and related conditions.
  • Hernia Repair: Surgical correction of abdominal and inguinal hernias to prevent complications.
  • Breast Biopsy: Diagnostic removal of a small breast tissue sample for cancer evaluation.
  • Lumpectomy: Targeted removal of breast tumors while preserving healthy tissue.
  • Hemorrhoidectomy: Surgical treatment for advanced or persistent hemorrhoids.
  • Anti-Reflux Surgery: Surgical management of GERD when medication is ineffective.
  • Laparoscopy: Minimally invasive surgical technique using small incisions and camera guidance.
  • Colon & Rectal Surgery: Treatment of colorectal diseases including cancer, IBD, and bleeding disorders.
  • Prostatectomy: Surgical removal of the prostate gland for cancer or severe obstruction.
  • Splenectomy: Removal of the spleen due to trauma, blood disorders, or enlargement.
  • Hysterectomy: Surgical removal of the uterus for fibroids, bleeding, or malignancy.

Orthopaedics

Our Orthopaedics department provides comprehensive care for bones, joints, ligaments, and spine conditions — from trauma and fracture management to advanced joint replacement and minimally invasive arthroscopic procedures for faster recovery and better mobility.

  • ACL/PCL Reconstruction Surgery: Ligament reconstruction to restore knee stability after sports or trauma injuries.
  • Hand Surgery: Treatment for fractures, tendon/nerve injuries, and common hand disorders to regain function.
  • All Types of Trauma: Emergency and definitive care for bone and joint injuries caused by accidents or falls.
  • Knee Replacement Surgery: Joint replacement for severe arthritis or damage to reduce pain and improve walking.
  • Shoulder Replacement Surgery: Replacement of damaged shoulder joint surfaces to restore motion and reduce pain.
  • Hip Replacement Surgery: Replacement of the hip joint for arthritis, fractures, or advanced joint degeneration.
  • Knee Arthroscopy: Minimally invasive keyhole surgery to diagnose and treat internal knee problems.
  • Shoulder Arthroscopy: Keyhole procedure for rotator cuff issues, instability, and other shoulder injuries.
  • Ankle Repair: Surgical management of ankle ligament injuries, fractures, and tendon problems.
  • Spinal Surgeries: Surgical treatment for selected spine problems such as compression, instability, or trauma.
  • Joint Fusion: Fusion of a painful joint to provide long-term stability and pain relief.
  • Fracture Fixation with Implants: Fixing fractures using plates, screws, rods, or other implants for proper healing.

Ophthalmology

Our Ophthalmology department offers advanced diagnostic and surgical care for eye disorders, focusing on vision preservation, correction, and restoration using modern surgical and laser techniques.

  • Laser Eye Surgery: Laser-based procedures to correct refractive errors and improve vision.
  • Cataract Surgery: Removal of cloudy natural lenses with implantation of artificial intraocular lenses.
  • Glaucoma Surgery: Surgical procedures to reduce eye pressure and prevent vision loss.
  • Refractive Surgery: Vision correction procedures for myopia, hyperopia, and astigmatism.
  • Corneal Surgery: Treatment of corneal diseases, injuries, and degenerations.
  • Vitreoretinal Surgery: Surgery for retinal detachment, diabetic retinopathy, and vitreous disorders.
  • Eye Muscle Surgery: Surgical correction for squint and eye alignment disorders.
  • Trauma Repair: Emergency surgical management of eye injuries due to accidents or trauma.
  • Oculoplastic Surgery: Reconstructive and cosmetic procedures involving eyelids and surrounding structures.
  • Eyelid Surgery: Surgical treatment for eyelid deformities affecting vision or appearance.
  • Entropion Repair: Correction of inward-turning eyelids to prevent corneal damage.
  • Ectropion Repair: Correction of outward-turning eyelids to protect the eye surface.

Medical / Conservative Treatment Facility

Our Medical department provides comprehensive non-surgical management for acute and chronic medical conditions, including infections, metabolic disorders, respiratory illnesses, and critical care emergencies, with continuous monitoring and evidence-based treatment.

  • Complicated Malaria: Intensive medical management of severe malaria and its complications.
  • All Types of Fever: Evaluation and treatment of viral, bacterial, and parasitic fevers.
  • Diabetes & Diabetic Ketoacidosis: Emergency and long-term management of uncontrolled diabetes.
  • Common & Severe Infections: Treatment of colds, flu, sinusitis, urinary tract, and systemic infections.
  • Abdominal Pain: Medical evaluation and stabilization of acute and chronic abdominal conditions.
  • High Blood Pressure: Management of hypertension and prevention of complications.
  • Severe Sepsis: Critical care management of life-threatening systemic infections.
  • Dengue Fever: Close monitoring and supportive care for dengue and related complications.
  • Acute Gastroenteritis: Treatment of severe dehydration due to vomiting and diarrhea.
  • Viral & Chronic Hepatitis: Medical management of acute and long-term liver diseases.
  • Skin & Soft Tissue Infections: Treatment of cellulitis, abscesses, and deep tissue infections.
  • Bronchiectasis: Long-term medical care for chronic airway disease.
  • Interstitial Lung Disease Exacerbation: Management of acute respiratory deterioration.
  • Chronic Pancreatitis: Medical treatment for pain control and digestive complications.
  • Severe Respiratory Conditions: Management of pneumonia, asthma, COPD, ARDS, and related disorders.
  • Seizures: Emergency and long-term treatment of seizure disorders.
  • Pneumothorax: Medical stabilization and monitoring of collapsed lung cases.
  • Neuromuscular Disorders: Medical care for muscle and nerve-related diseases.
  • Accelerated Hypertension: Emergency control of dangerously high blood pressure.
